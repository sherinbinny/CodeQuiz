# Code Quiz

### Description

Welcome to the Coding Quiz Challenge! This is a simple web-based quiz game designed to test your knowledge of coding concepts and languages.

## Features

- **Timer**: A countdown timer starts when you begin the quiz. Each question must be answered within the allotted time. Incorrect answers will penalize your remaining time.
- **Questions**: Answer a series of code-related questions presented one at a time. Choose the correct answer from multiple choices.
- **Feedback**: Receive immediate feedback after answering each question, indicating whether your answer was correct or incorrect.
- **End of Game**: The game ends when all questions are answered or when the timer reaches zero. At this point, your final score is displayed.
- **Save High Scores**: After completing the quiz, you can enter your initials to save your score. High scores are stored locally and can be viewed on the Highscores page.


## How to Play

1. Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin.
2. Read each question carefully and select your answer.
3. If your answer is correct, you'll receive positive feedback. If not, time will be deducted from the clock.
4. Continue answering questions until the quiz ends.
5. After the quiz, enter your initials to save your score and see how you compare to other players on the Highscores page.
